# Contributing to Awesome AI Meeting Notes

First off, thank you for considering contributing to this curated list! It's people like you who make this a great resource for everyone.

## How Can I Contribute?

### Adding a New Tool

1.  **Search the list**: Ensure the tool isn't already there.
2.  **Fork the repository**.
3.  **Add the entry**:
    *   Place it in the correct category (SaaS or Open-Source).
    *   Maintain alphabetical order if possible.
    *   Include: Name, Link, and a concise 1-2 sentence description.
    *   Focus on **AI meeting transcription, summarization, and agentic features**.
4.  **Submit a Pull Request**: Provide a brief explanation of why you think this tool should be added.

### Reporting Inactive Links or Outdated Info

If you find a link that is broken or information that is no longer accurate, please open an issue or submit a PR to fix it.

## Contribution Guidelines

*   **Be Factual**: Keep descriptions objective and feature-focused.
*   **Quality over Quantity**: We aim for a curated list of high-quality, active projects.
*   **Formatting**: Follow the existing Markdown structure.

## Code of Conduct

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

Thank you for your help!
